PLAW's PET RESCUE

Here at PLAw's Pet Rescue, we are more than an adoption center. We are the middlemen connecting pets to their forever family, sharing love, joy and good-spirit all around.

Our Vision

We envision a a world filled with whole people with pets in each home, sharing love throughout the world.


Our Mission

We are dedicated to saving and rehabilitating as many lost, harmed or abused pets as possible, and conneting them to the right family, where both they and their new family can experience love in its realest state.


Our Core Values

  • Transparency
  • Love
  • Respect
  • Pet Priority, etc.
